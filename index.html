    We are the Fox Valley Python User Group. We meet on the first Thursday of the month, in Oshkosh, WI.

    Meetings

    The Fox Valley Python User Group meets on the first Thursday of the month, from 7 pm to 8:30 pm. We meet at the Oshkosh Public Library, in the conference room in the basement.

    We focus on the Python programming language, and the many libraries and frameworks written in Python. We welcome all people, of different skill levels and interests. Come learn and share your knowledge.
