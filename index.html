    We are the Fox Valley Python User Group. We meet on the first Monday of the month, in Oshkosh, WI.

    Meetings

    The Fox Valley Python User Group meets on the first Monday of the month, from 7 pm to 8:30 pm. We meet in Sage Hall, room 4422, on the UW-Oshkosh campus.That’s the GIS computer lab in Sage Hall, on the fourth floor.

    Parking

    Parking is available on High Avenue, in front of the Axle Tech factory. It costs 75¢/hr. Don’t park in the Sage Hall lot without a University parking permit.
